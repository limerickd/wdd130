Hey there fellow Sayer! Welcome to your tab. Here is where you can manage your presets on the messages you leave. Remember this is tied to your cache only, since you are not required to make an account. So, you can create some presets like colors, text size, fonts, and even link an image from another website, so you can use it as a footer or whatever your imagination tells you. Keep in mind that we don’t store the resources directly in our website. Everything is used via links. If an element you used gets taken down from its source page, it will not longer appear here. Also, changes you make on your tab are client based, the sever won’t save anything on our side. I wish we could do more, but we would have to make an offering to the internet gods first.
